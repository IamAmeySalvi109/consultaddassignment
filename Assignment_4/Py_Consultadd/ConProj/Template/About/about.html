SUPERNATURAL

This haunting series follows the thrilling journeys of Sam and Dean Winchester, two brothers who face an increasingly sinister landscape as they hunt monsters. After losing their mother to a supernatural force, the brothers were raised by their father as soldiers who track mysterious and demonic creatures. The brothers must rely on each other as they encounter all things that goes bump in the night.

Supernatural is an American dark fantasy television series created by Eric Kripke. It was first broadcast on September 13, 2005, on The WB, and subsequently became part of successor The CW's lineup. It stars Jared Padalecki as Sam Winchester, Jensen Ackles as Dean Winchester and Misha Collins as the angel, Castiel.

The series is filmed in Vancouver, British Columbia and surrounding areas. Originally, Kripke planned the series for three seasons but later expanded it to five. With its eleventh season, Supernatural became the longest-running American live-action fantasy TV series. The series has been renewed for a fifteenth and final season to consist of 20 episodes, which is set to premiere on October 10, 2019.
